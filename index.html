Tasty favourites.

Stirred cloudberries

Combine 500 grams of cloudberries with 150 grams sugar.

Pickled tiny mushrooms

Brush the mushrooms and get some clean containers for pickeling. Bring 1 part apple cider vinegar, 2 parts sugar, 3 pars water and a dash of salt to a boil. Add the mushroom and let it boil for a minute. Add some black peppercorns and juniper berries. Transfer to the container. Let it cool down and put it in your fridge.
